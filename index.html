FAQs

Quick answers before you reach out.

Can I sponsor or exhibit at an upcoming event?

Yes — most of our expos and networking events have sponsorship and exhibitor packages available. Get in touch and we'll match you with the right opportunity.

How do I get tickets to a GCDS event?

Check Upcoming Events above or visit our Events page — most sessions are free to attend, some require registration in advance.

Can GCDS plan a custom corporate event or retreat?

Yes. We plan bespoke corporate events, offsites, and retreats end-to-end — venue, logistics, and guest experience included.

Do you work outside the Illawarra region?

Yes — while we're based in the Illawarra, we work locally and internationally wherever the right partnership takes us.
