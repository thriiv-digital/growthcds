Our Story

Growth Concept Development Services (GCDS) was founded by Luke Griffin, who's spent over 25 years connecting people, projects, and purpose across the events, exhibitions, and partnerships landscape.

About Luke

Luke is a local dad with two kids under 10, based in the Hornsby area for the past eight years. He grew up in Castlecrag, surrounded by nature and a strong sense of community, and spent much of his early career life between the pubs of Balmain and the close-knit neighbourhoods around Wollstonecraft — places that shaped his appreciation for connection, collaboration, and genuine relationships.

A graduate of St Aloysius' College and the Blue Mountains International Hotel Management School, Luke's early training built a foundation of professionalism, hospitality, and people-focused leadership that still guides his work today.

When he's not developing partnerships or delivering events, you'll find him running, playing golf, or testing his strategy at the poker table.

A long-time advocate for health and wellbeing, Luke has recently focused on fitness and community participation — completing several fun runs, including a half marathon.
