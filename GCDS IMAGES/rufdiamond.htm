Canada's Leading Supplier of Equipment for Remote Industrial Operations

RufDiamond LTD supplies the Fat Truck® amphibious all-terrain vehicle, IronHorse® tracked transport systems, Agilis® Mobile Batch Plants, and Batt Pack® hybrid power systems to support mining, utilities, forestry, emergency response, defence, construction, and remote industrial operations across Canada.

Move People, Materials, and Power Where Roads Don’t Go

From muskeg, wetlands, deep snow, rocky terrain, and underground operations to remote off-grid worksites, RufDiamond provides purpose-built equipment that helps organizations improve access, reduce downtime, enhance safety, and keep critical operations moving.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about RufDiamond's products, industries, support services, and how we help organizations operate in Canada's most challenging environments.

What products does RufDiamond offer?

RufDiamond supplies Fat Truck® amphibious all-terrain vehicles, IronHorse® tracked transport systems, Agilis® Mobile Batch Plants, Batt Pack® hybrid power systems, and genuine OEM parts for remote industrial and commercial operations across Canada.

What industries does RufDiamond serve?

RufDiamond supports organizations in the mining, utilities, forestry, construction, emergency response, and defencesectors with specialized equipment designed for remote access, challenging terrain, and off-grid operations.

Where is RufDiamond located?

RufDiamond is headquartered in Coniston, Ontario, and serves customers across Canada through a network of authorized dealers and industry partners.

How can I request a quote?

You can request a quote by completing our Contact Us form or by reaching out to our team directly. We'll help you find the right equipment solution for your project and connect you with the appropriate dealer or sales representative.
